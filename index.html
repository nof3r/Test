What is a Haplogroup?

A haplogroup is a group of people who share a common ancestor on the direct paternal (Y-DNA) line. Root haplogroups like R, E, T, and J represent major branches in the human Y-DNA tree.

Click a root haplogroup to see its major subclades. Each subclade shows countries with highest frequency and useful tree links.
